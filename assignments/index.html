Rollship

The purpose of this object is to connect close human beings through brainwaves (EEG) using as a medium an everyday rolling ball.

Each ball can be rolled by you or by any person with its access hash key.

Let's say Bob has a ball (LHS) and Alice is his friend (RHS). When, Alice wears an EEG headset and sends commands using brainwaves to Bob via a distributed network, the ball that Bob has (LHS) will start moving and emiting a particular color attributed to her access key.

Three magical powers:
1. Telepathy
2. Feel of closeness
3. Moving objects with thoughts
